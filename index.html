About

Supporting NOAA as a Junior Scientist, I specialize in the processing and verification of SAR acquisitions over active hurricanes. I bring a strong technical focus to the role by writing Python scripts to automate analytical processing chains, while also leveraging ArcGIS Pro to translate raw data into professional-grade spatial visualizations.

Projects

Georgia Coast Atlas: Wolf and Egg Islands

This undergraduate research project was a collaboration with the Emory University Center for Digital Scholarship (ECDS) to obtain data and quantify shoreline change on a Georgia barrier island called Wolf Island. This project is featured on the Georgia Coast Atlas website, has been presented at two conferences, and was featured in a news article.
